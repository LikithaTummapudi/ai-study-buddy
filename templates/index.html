About

AI Study Buddy is an AI-powered learning assistant designed to help students study more effectively and independently. Traditional learning resources often provide lengthy explanations or require significant time to search through multiple sources. AI Study Buddy simplifies this process by delivering instant concept explanations, concise note summaries, practice quizzes, and flashcards tailored to the student's needs. By leveraging generative AI,the platform aims to make learning more accessible, engaging, and efficient.
